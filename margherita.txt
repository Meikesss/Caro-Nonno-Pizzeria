Margherita Pizza:

Pizza dough
Pizza sauce
Fresh mozzarella cheese
Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Fresh basil leaves

Preparation:
Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape.
Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly on the dough.
Tear or slice the fresh mozzarella cheese and distribute it over the sauce.
Drizzle some olive oil on top and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden.
Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and serve hot.
