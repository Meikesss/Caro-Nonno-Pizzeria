Pepperoni Pizza:

Ingredients:

Pizza dough
Pizza sauce
Pepperoni slices
Shredded mozzarella cheese
Optional: Sliced black olives or sliced bell peppers

Preparation:

Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape.
Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly on the dough.
Arrange pepperoni slices over the sauce. Add sliced black olives or bell peppers if desired.
Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the toppings.
Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and bake the pizza for approximately 12-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.
Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
